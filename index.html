Built a real-time aircraft flight simulator in MATLAB using full 6-DOF rigid body dynamics. Implemented quaternion-based attitude representation for stable 3D orientation, combined aerodynamic, propulsive, and gravitational force models, and used a 4th-order Runge–Kutta integrator for real-time stability. Live pilot inputs (elevator & aileron) streamed from an Arduino joystick, with 3D visualization of aircraft motion.

Failure Analysis FEA (SolidWorks) Fracture Mechanics

Forensics of a Fridge Handle

Forensic analysis of a fractured ABS refrigerator handle. Identified fatigue failure from cyclic tensile, compressive, and torsional loading. SolidWorks FEA confirmed peak Von Mises stress of 166 psi at the exact fracture site under only 8 lbf. Modeled fracture toughness vs. crack propagation and recommended Nylon 6 + 30% glass fiber as a 160% stronger, cost-competitive alternative.
